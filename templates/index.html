Our 6-Month Roadmap

Here’s our step-by-step plan to raise awareness, grow our campaign, and take meaningful action against water pollution.

Month 1: Awareness Launch

Launch website and social media pages. Start spreading the word about the dangers of water pollution.

Month 2: Local Engagement

Organize local clean-up events, school talks, and partnerships with environmental clubs.

Month 3: Campaign Expansion

Start “Adopt a River” and “Plastic-Free Challenge” campaigns to increase involvement.

Month 4: Policy Push

Launch petitions and send letters to local governments to enforce water protection laws.

Month 5: Education Tools

Release toolkits for students and teachers about clean water, with videos and guides.

Month 6: Global Day of Action

Host a global online event showcasing clean water heroes and projects around the world.
